PPFantasy

I can't stop thinking about the end of the world,

Romanticized images of a post-apocalyptic fantasy,

The yearning to be reduced to my most primal.

I expect then my demons will become tiny, insignificant,

Like they do in the ocean,

Or when facing a big mountain or a huge sub.

I've been scavenging for answers:

Who am I? What am I?

Beyond the words that define this momentary and volatile identity.

And of course, in the absurdity of this question, you could waste a lifetime.

So after this, I promise to stop…
